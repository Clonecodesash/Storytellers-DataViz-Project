CO2 emissions around the world - Bar charts

This section aims to analyze and visualize data of per capita CO2 emissions all around the world. It includes 5 different plots to compare the emissions of different countries and regions.

Data Source: Global Carbon Budget (2023); Population based on various sources (2023)

Distribution of CO2 emissions - Alluvial chart

This section aims to illustrate the distribution of CO2 emission across the world. It includes an alluvial plot to visualize the distribution of the emissions of different countries and regions.

Data Source: Global Carbon Budget (2023); Population based on various sources (2023)

Global CO2 emissions - Maps

This section aims to illustrate the distribution of CO2 emission across the world using maps. It includes 4 map plots to visualize the distribution of the emissions of different countries.

Data Source: Global Carbon Budget (2023); Population based on various sources (2023)

State-wide Temperature Data - Timelines and Distributions

This section aims to illustrate the distribution of temperature in Maryland across several years using charts. It includes 3 plots of linechart, radarchart and ridgeline to visualize the timeline and distribution data.

Data Source: NOAA National Centers for Environmental information, Climate at a Glance: Statewide Time Series, published December 2024
